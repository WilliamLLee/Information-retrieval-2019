个人简历：

I am currently an Associate Professor of Philosophy at Nankai University in Tianjin (China). My research interests focus on philosophy of social science, particularly methodological individualism, hermeneutics, fallibilism, ordinary rationality, complex systems, enactivism and Austrian School of Economics.
