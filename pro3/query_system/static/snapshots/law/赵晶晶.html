赵晶晶 讲师

教育背景：

英国思克莱德大学，法学博士（PhD）

英国爱丁堡大学，法学硕士（LLM）

中国政法大学，法学学士


研究领域：国际经济法，国际环境法


荣誉奖励及专业资格：

英国思克莱德大学，坎贝尔.伯恩斯基金（全额博士奖学金）

英国思克莱德大学，休金基金

中国政法大学，校级一等奖学金

英国高等教育学会，会员


学术成果：

（一）专著

Reconciling Trade and Environmental Conflicts in International Law：the WTO Agreements and the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety （Routledge, 2020，forthcoming）.

（二）论文

1. ‘Towards State Avoidance of Conflicts between the SPS Agreement and the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety: A Fresh Perspective’, in Vol. 53.4 Journal of World Trade (SSCI) (2019), 625-646.

2. ‘Forest Law and Concerns on Biodiversity and Sustainable Development’ in Issue 8, IUCN Academy of International Law eJournal.

3. “我国转基因产品监管公众参与制度研究”，载于文轩主编：《生态文明语境下的环境资源法制研究》，中国政法大学出版社2016年版，第189~211页。

4. ‘The Revised Atmospheric Pollution Prevention and Control Law’ in Issue 7, IUCN Academy of International Law eJournal.

5. ‘Public Interest Environmental Litigation and the Revised Environmental Protection Law of the People’s Republic of China,’in Issue 6, IUCN Academy of International Law eJournal.

（三）会议报告

1. ‘Towards State Avoidance of Conflicts between the SPS Agreement and the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety’, 8th Conference of the Postgraduate and Early Professionals/Academics Network of the Society of International Economic Law, May 2019, London, UK.

2.‘Avoiding Conflicts between International Trade and Environmental Treaties: Beyond the Systemic Integration Principle’, Law and the Environment 2017, Cork, Ireland.

3.‘Examining the Relationship between the WTO Agreements and the Cartagena Protocol from a Norm Conflict Perspective’, SCELG Invited Seminar, November 2016, Glasgow, UK.
